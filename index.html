How to Play
  • Click "Start Game" to start the game.
  • On the next section, choose your move - Rock, Paper, or Scissors
    Note that you only have 10 seconds to make your choice or you fail!
  • If you don't choose your move within 10 seconds, you fail!
    If you do fail, then just click on the "Restart Game" button to go back to the choice screen.
  • After you choose your move, the computer will make theirs and you will be shown the result Page.
  • Have fun and good luck!
